TOTO SAH SHRINE

Toto Sah Apparatus | Dueling Peaks Region

TUTORIAL

  1. Toto Sah shrine is hidden behind a rock wall on the bank of a river southeast of Dueling Peaks Tower. The easiest way to destroy the rock wall is to swim to a rock outcropping upriver, toss a remote bomb into the river and, when it floats in front of the wall, detonate the bomb.
  2. Using motion controls, rotate the first puzzle so that it creates a bridge to the next platform. Open the treasure chest to get a Shield of the Mind’s Eye Shield (Guard Up +20).
  3. Rotate the next puzzle so that the three staircases are all in the middle, and they create a path to the next platform.
  4. Rotate the next puzzle to expose the treasure chest with a small key. Get the key and return to the control mechanism.
  5. Rotate the same puzzle again to create a path to the next platform. The stairs should be to the right and close to you.
  6. Meet Toto Sah, and collect your spirit orb.
