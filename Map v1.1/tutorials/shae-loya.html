SHAE LOYA SHRINE

Aim for the Moment | Ridgeland Region

TUTORIAL

  1. Use Stasis to freeze the boulder, then shoot it with a few arrows while it’s frozen in time. That’ll cause it to roll into its bowl and activate a launcher that throws you up into the air.
  2. Stand on the launcher, fly into the air, deploy your paraglider, and fly straight ahead.
  3. Stand on the second launcher, and reach the treasure chest for a chunk of topaz.
  4. Shoot the ropes holding up a second treasure chest for a Falcon Bow (Quick Shot).
  5. Make your way back up to the first launcher, ride it up into the air, turn left, and shoot the orange switch with an arrow. That’ll lift the gate and open the exit.
  6. Go through the exit, meet Shae Loya, and collect your spirit orb.
