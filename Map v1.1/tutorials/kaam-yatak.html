KAAM YA'TAK SHRINE

Trial of Power | Central Region

TUTORIAL

  1. Use Magnesis to move the boulder in a tube to the opposite side of where it’s resting. Let go, and the boulder on a chain will smash into and open the door.
  2. Run through the next section, using Stasis or Magnesis as necessary to avoid the spiked boulders.
  3. Kill the Guardian Scouts.
  4. Use your bow and arrow to cut the rope holding the lantern, so it falls and ignites the foliage. (Or you can ignite a regular arrow on the torch to create a fire arrow and launch that at the foliage.) That’ll burn the platform on which the boulder sits, causing it to roll and open a door to the next area.
  5. Before you run away, open the treasure chest near the boulder’s platform for an Edge of Duality.
  6. Walk through the doors that opened, turn left and open the next treasure chest to get a Royal Broadsword (Long Throw).
  7. Follow the only path available to you, defeat two more Guardian Scouts, and use Stasis on the seesaw part of the floor that will give way if you try to walk across it.
  8. Interact with the Sheikah slate terminal, and use motion controls to slam the hammer into the boulder. It’ll fall down the ramp and open the next doors.
  9. Before you leave, open the treasure chest near the terminal to recieve an Ancient Core.
  10. Continue down the path, use Stasis on the seesaw and walk across it to the other side.
  11. Use an arrow to burn the foliage (and therefore the platform) on which the boulder rests. There’s a torch here you can use to ignite your arrow.
  12. Cast Stasis on the seesaw again, and step on the pressure plate to lift the ramp and cause the boulder to fly across the gap, roll across the seesaw, and open yet another door.
  13. Stand on the pressure plate to open the gate, and use Stasis on the battering ram on rails. It’ll fly and open the last set of doors.
  14. Before you leave the area, look for a wall of cracked blocks. Destroy them with a remote bomb to uncover a treasure chest containing a Silver Rupee.
  15. Walk through the last set of doors, stand in front of the switch, hit the switch with a weapon, and glide onto the area that’s higher than where the switch is but not as high as the column on which the monk sits. Walk around to the back, and you’ll find a treasure chest with a diamond.
  16. Hit the crystal switch again to catapult up, glide to the top of the highest column, meet Kaam Ya’tak and collect your spirit orb.
