MOGG LATAN SHRINE

Synced Swing | Ridgeland Region

TUTORIAL

  1. Use Magnesis to make the two platforms on chains rock, so that you can walk onto them and exit on the next sturdy platforms.
  2. Use your bow and arrow to cut the strings holding the drawbridge up. Walk across and open the chest to find a Forest Dweller's Spear (Durability Up).
  3. Use Magnesis to move the spiked boulders on chains, so you can walk to the end of the plank and open the treasure chest for a Forest Dwellers Bow (Quick Shot).
  4. Backtrack, walk up the steps, and use magnesis to pull the platforms on rails toward you, so that you create a makeshift staircase.
  5. At the end of the third platform on rails, jump off paraglide and below your destination, where youâ€™ll find a treasure chest with a gold rupee (worth 300 rupees).
  6. Use Magnesis to create a staircase back to where you started.
  7. Recreate the staircase you first made, and exit onto solid ground.
  8. Either hit the switch to reveal lanterns and sever their ropes with an arrow, or just shoot the foliage with a fire arrow to burn away the foliage.
  9. Use Magnesis to open the doors at the top of the ramp.
  10. Meet Mogg Latan and collect your spirit orb.
